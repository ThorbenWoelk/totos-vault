# Psychoneuroimmunology (PNI)

Psychoneuroimmunology is the study of the interaction between psychological processes and the nervous and immune systems of the human body.

## Key Mechanisms
- **Bidirectional Communication**: The brain and immune system communicate with each other through signaling pathways (e.g., cytokines).
- **Stress Response**: Chronic stress can suppress immune function, increasing susceptibility to infections and slowing wound healing.
- **Inflammation**: Psychological factors like depression and stress can trigger systemic inflammation, which is linked to various chronic diseases.
