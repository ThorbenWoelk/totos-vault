# The Bayesian Brain

The "Bayesian brain" hypothesis argues that the brain implements a form of Bayesian inference to perceive the world and make decisions.

## Predictive Coding
- **Prediction**: The brain constantly generates predictions about sensory input based on prior models.
- **Prediction Error**: The difference between the prediction and actual sensory input.
- **Update**: The brain updates its internal models to minimize prediction error.

## Relevance
- Explains how **[[Perception|Perception]]** is constructive.
- Hallucinations in **[[../Psychotic Disorders/Schizophrenia]]** may be due to overly strong priors.
