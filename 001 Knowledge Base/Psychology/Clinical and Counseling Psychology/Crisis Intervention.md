# Crisis Intervention

Crisis intervention is a time-limited intervention with a specific psychotherapeutic approach to immediately stabilize those in crisis.

## Key Goals
1.  **Safety**: Ensure the physical and psychological safety of the client and others.
2.  **Stabilization**: Return the client to their pre-crisis level of functioning.
3.  **Resources**: Connect the client with support systems and resources.

## Suicide Prevention
- **Assessment**: Directly asking about suicidal ideation, plan, and intent.
- **Intervention**: Creating a safety plan, removing access to lethal means.
- **Risk Factors**: Previous attempts, mental illness (e.g., [[../Mood Disorders/Depression]]), hopelessness.
