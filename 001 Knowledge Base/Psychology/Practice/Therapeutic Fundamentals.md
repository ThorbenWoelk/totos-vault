# Common Factors Theory

 Since "specific techniques" (e.g., cognitive restructuring vs. exploring childhood patterns) generally yield the same results, the curative power is attributed to factors shared across all therapies:

1. **Therapeutic Alliance:** The quality of the bond between client and therapist remains the strongest predictor of outcome.
2. **Empathy and Positive Regard:** The therapist's ability to provide a supportive environment.
