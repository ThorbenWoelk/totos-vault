#ai-coding #best-practices
NEVER trust an AI to give you correct URL links. 
Mistake I made: letting AI download data without checking where the data came from and what the data actually was. The links provided led nowhere. The processing had no validation. The AI (`gemini 3 flash`) then hallucinated URLs. I could never verify origin of data and had to delete all, start from scratch.
